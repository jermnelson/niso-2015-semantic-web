BIBFRAME 2.0

In the fall of 2015, the Library of Congress released a series of proposals for changing and updating the BIBFRAME vocabulary to version 2.0. These draft proposals include:

  • BIBFRAME Title
  • BIBFRAME Agents and Roles
  • BIBFRAME Items
  • BIBFRAME Events
  • BIBFRAME Identifiers and Notes
  • BIBFRAME Administrative Metadata
  • BIBFRAME Categories
